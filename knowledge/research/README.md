# knowledge/research/

This folder holds persistent research notes — material that is longer-lived than a single spike session and worth keeping across weeks.

---

## What belongs here

- Research findings on tools, frameworks, patterns, or approaches that are not tied to a single week
- Reference material distilled from reading or investigation
- Background context that informs multiple projects or planning decisions
- Comparative notes (option A vs. option B) that are not yet decisions

---

## Distinction from `04_LOGS/Spike_Log.md`

| | `04_LOGS/Spike_Log.md` | `knowledge/research/` |
|---|---|---|
| **Scope** | Specific spike outcome (time-boxed, one task) | Longer-lived research material |
| **Format** | Spike entry with duration, question, finding | Free-form notes, references, summaries |
| **Tied to a week?** | Yes — spike is scheduled and completed | No — can accumulate over time |
| **Binding?** | No — informs planning | No — informs planning and design |

---

## Promotion rule

If research here becomes a system rule or operating principle:
- Promote to the relevant section in `01_OS/operating_system_thang_nguyen_v1_1.md`
- Or record a decision in `04_LOGS/ADR/`
- Or create a reusable summary in `knowledge/summaries/`

Research notes here are informational, not prescriptive. Do not treat them as rules until explicitly promoted.
