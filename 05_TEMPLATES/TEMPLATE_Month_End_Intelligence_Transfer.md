# TEMPLATE_Month_End_Intelligence_Transfer.md — Monthly Planning Context Bridge

> **Month:** YYYY-MM (e.g., 2026-04)  
> **Created:** YYYY-MM-DD (date after Monthly Review Exit Gate passes)  
> **Input source:** Monthly Review + Decision Log + Daily/Weekly patterns + External signals  
> **Output:** Planning context for next month (not decisions; not commitments)  
> **Time budget:** 20–30 min  
> **Audience:** Planner (same person or different person creating next month's plan)

---

## TEMPLATE PURPOSE

This artifact bridges the month-end process by transforming backward-looking findings into forward-looking planning context.

### What this is:
- Selection of high-signal items from Monthly Review that should influence next month
- Organized planning inputs (not planning decisions)
- Operator-independent context artifact (allows planning handoff)
- Single-responsibility: "What context shapes next-month planning?"

### What this is NOT:
- A retrospective (that's Monthly Review)
- A plan or commitment (that's Monthly Plan)
- A complete analysis (lightweight, high-signal only)
- A decision log (that's existing Decision Log)
- A place for exploration (keep entries specific to planning)

---
